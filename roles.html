Party Structure

Roles and responsibilities within Rashtra Shaktibadi Party.

Organisational Roles
#PositionCategoryResponsibilities
1PresidentLeadershipHighest authority of the party. Presides over all meetings, decisions, and represents the party at the national level.
2Vice PresidentLeadershipAssists the President, acts in the President's absence, and oversees specific portfolios as assigned.
3SecretaryLeadershipManages day-to-day administrative functions, correspondence, and official communications of the party.
4General SecretaryLeadershipOversees organisational activities, coordinates between units, and ensures implementation of party resolutions.
5TreasurerLeadershipManages party finances, maintains accounts, oversees fundraising, and presents financial reports to the committee.
6Executive MembersExecutiveForm the executive committee; participate in policy discussions, vote on party decisions, and lead local chapters.
7MemberGeneralRegistered members of the party who participate in activities, campaigns, and support party initiatives.
