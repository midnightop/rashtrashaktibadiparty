Code of Rules

Non-negotiable guidelines binding every registered volunteer and senior cadre administrator.

01

Financial Cleanliness Protocol

Every structural allocation or collection must balance inside our transparent decentralized portal. Unvouched monetary transactions mean instant removal.

02

Zero Communal Provocation

Political strategy or dynamic speeches must remain targeted purely on administrative frameworks, growth parameters, and public balance. Hate speech is instantly prohibited.

03

Command Sync Guidelines

District or ground coordinators cannot broadcast individual official positions or issue legal press statements without clear written verification from the central desk.
