Rules of Joining

Membership eligibility criteria and code of conduct.

Membership Rules
1

Age Requirement

The applicant must be a minimum of 18 years of age.

2

Indian Citizenship

Membership is open exclusively to citizens of India.

3

Single Party Membership

An applicant must not be an active member of any other registered political party.

4

Code of Conduct

Members must conduct themselves with dignity. Hate speech or anti-national activity is prohibited.
